Djerba

Explore the Largest Island in North Africa

Overview

Djerba, situated off the coast of Tunisia, is a beautiful island in the Mediterranean Sea. With historical roots in Greek mythology (think Odysseus and the Lotus Eaters), Djerba has been a hidden paradise for a thousand years.

Beaches and Coastal Beauty

Djerba boasts stunning beaches with crystal-clear waters. Whether you're sunbathing, swimming, or enjoying water sports, the coastal beauty is unmatched.

Explore the Medina

Wander through the narrow streets of Houmt Souk, the main town on Djerba. The medina offers colorful markets, traditional architecture, and local crafts.
